01 — About

A little about me

22 June 1999 • Dhaka, BD

Born in Dhaka, Bangladesh in 1999, I'm currently a 3rd-year Computer Science & Engineering student at American International University Bangladesh (AIUB).

Outside of code, I live for stories — telling them, writing them, filming them. I'm equally at home strumming a few chords on a guitar, sinking hours into a strange video game, or showing up for the volunteer communities that shaped me.

I'm a kid with a million dreams and, somehow, a centillion ideas left to chase.
